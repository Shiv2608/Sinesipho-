ABOUT US

Anathi Organisation Center is based at Emaplazini NPO 225-688 . We
work for the whole INK area and work in partners with Ikhethelo
Youth Development Society in collaboration with Department of Social Development ,
Department of health , EThekwini Municipality and other civil society organisations
and other partners who have a mandate to respond to government priorities through
empowering young people especially women who were previously disadvantaged
to be self-sustainable and be employable and thus start up their entrepreneurship projects.

WE GIVE HOPE TO THOSE AFFECTED BY HIV/AIDS

WE ALSO TUTOR THE YOUTH ABOUT HUMAN ILLNESSES
